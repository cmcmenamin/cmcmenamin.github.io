Digital for every human

OpenAccess is an Aotearoa-based consultancy that helps to make the digital world more accessible for disabled people.

Services

OpenAccess works with teams and organisations to improve the accessibility of their digital products and services.

Whether you're a small business, a government department, or a not-for-profit, we can help you to make your digital products and services accessible to everyone.

Services include:

  • Accessibility audits
  • Training and workshops
  • Design and development support
  • Strategy and policy development
  • Testing and quality assurance advice
  • Accessibility statements
  • Procurement support
  • Bespoke accessible software development

Pricing

Accessibility should never be unaffordable.

Pricing is tailored to the needs of each client. Contact us, and we'll work to find the right solution that balances your budget with your accessibility goals.

Discounted rates

OpenAccess offers discounted rates for the following groups:

  • Disability organisations
  • Museums, galleries, and libraries
  • Not-for-profits
  • Small businesses (less than 5 employees)

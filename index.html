Digital for every human

OpenAccess is an Aotearoa-based consultancy that helps to make the digital world more accessible for disabled people.

Services

OpenAccess works with teams and organisations to improve the accessibility of their digital products and services.

Accessibility is all about removing barriers, so everyone can use and enjoy digital content.

Services include:

  • Accessibility audits
  • Training and workshops
  • Design and development support
  • Strategy and policy development
  • Testing and quality assurance process advice
  • Automated testing setup
  • Procurement support

Our experience

OpenAccess is led by Callum McMenamin, a digital accessibility specialist with over a decade of experience in digital, and 5+ years in accessibility.

Callum has low vision, and this lived experience of disability informs his work.

Previously, Callum worked as a Web Standards Consultant at the Deparment of Internal Affairs, where he helped to develop New Zealand Government's official Web Accessibility Guide, and contributed to the development of New Zealand Government's future accessibility standards.

If you are a New Zealand Government agency, OpenAccess is uniquely placed to help you meet your New Zealand Government Web Standards obligations.
